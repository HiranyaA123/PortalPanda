Our Story

Fire, flour & a whole lotta cheese

Needa Pizza started in a tiny Aberfoyle Park shopfront with one stone oven and a stubborn belief: real pizza takes real time. We still cold-proof our dough for two days, crush our own tomatoes, and fire every pie at 400°C until the crust chars just right.

No frozen bases. No premade sauce. Just proper pizza, made by people who actually eat it.
