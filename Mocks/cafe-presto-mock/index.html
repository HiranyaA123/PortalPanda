Feature · Local

Coffee, cake & a good sit on Glen Osmond Road.

Café Presto opened its doors on the Arkaba Village strip with a plain idea: make everything from scratch, roast your own beans, keep the cabinet full, and let the neighbourhood settle in. Six mornings a week, we're behind the machine before seven — pulling shots, plating eggs and pouring out the day's first flat whites for the regulars who've already parked out front.

The menu leans modern Australian — smashed avo, hotcakes, a burger you'll think about later — with an espresso program that gets as much attention as the food. We roast a fresh batch every Wednesday. Bags on the shelf, whole bean or ground how you like.

02. The Coffee · Wednesday's Batch

Roasted in-house, every Wednesday.

Coffee is the whole reason we opened the door. Green beans arrive from three farms — Colombian Huila, Ethiopian Sidamo, Brazilian Cerrado — and we roast a fresh batch every Wednesday morning.

Grab a bag at the counter — whole bean, plunger, filter, or espresso ground to order. 250g, 500g and 1kg. Ask for the current batch card.

— The Presto roasters
